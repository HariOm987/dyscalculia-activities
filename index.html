Dyscalculia—Dyslexia for Math


Dyscalculia is a disease similar in nature to the better known dyslexia—but for math. People with Dyscalculia often have trouble learning and understanding arithmetic and have poor number sense. Unfortunately, this disease lasts a lifetime and requires lots of work to combat.

Do I have dyscalculia?

While only 3-5% of the population is estimated to have dyscalculia, there is a very real possibility that you may be in that 3-5%. This test will evaluate whether you have dyscalculia.
